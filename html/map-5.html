    Chapter 3: The Culmination
    "Facing Reality"

    The search for information about the pendant continues. Eavesdropping on students, examining it, and asking others leaves you feeling incomplete. You notice the pendant's runes resemble the text above the school entrance. As you explore for clues, the pressure mounts, and the pendant pulses stronger. Suddenly, it glows and warms in your hand. Making you anxious and creating a sense of urgency to hurry. You begin to doubt yourself...

    "Should I keep searching for clues or I should probably just give up? I'm no Prof. Curio and this is more difficult than I thought it would be."
