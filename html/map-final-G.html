The New Life Awaits

Chapter 4: The End!
"A New Beginning!"

With help from your friends and the research you've conducted, you manage to figure out how to harness the pendant's magic. In a final, climactic moment, you open the Chamber of Origin, and visually see a river of purple magically essence surge into the pendant. The pendant glows fiercly. Since you are the one holding it, it bonds to you and transfers years of knowledge allowing this newfound intellect to enhance your own magical abilities. The power of the pendant is now yours to wield responsibly.

Your newfound abilities now allow you for incredible possibilities that you know you must use with responsibly. While you aren't the strongest wizard, you are still tenfold stronger than you were before.
