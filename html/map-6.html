    Chapter 3: The Culmination
    "Facing Issues"

    You’ve uncovered a theory in Professor Curio's book that outlines how to awaken the pendant’s power and where to go. But unlocking such potent magic is perilous, with many ways it could go awry. As you read on, a note at the bottom catches your eye:

    “Hello, Me. As I research the Chamber of Origins, I’m still searching for the key. I know about the Chambers and their location in the Forbidden Forest; it involves a pendant. While I’m confident in my findings, there’s still much to learn and work on. High hopes for finding that key soon!”
